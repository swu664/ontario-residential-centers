Our Team

To provide the very best services to the clients we serve, our residential centers ensures our team of professionals are trained and supervised.

Our team of staff and consultants include:

  • Board Certified Behaviour Analyst designs and supervises all clinical programs & services to help reduce challenging behaviours and teach appropiate skills.
  • Registered Behaviour Technicians serve as a part of the lead staff on each team and are certified to provide behavioural support.
  • Psychologists consult on client care and oversee programs that require additional assessments and treatment planning.
  • Psychiatrist collaborate with psychologists to ensure the medical and psychological well-being of our clients.
  • Abuse Victim Counselors & Crisis Workers bring specialized training and empathy to help individuals navigate emergency and crisis situations with safety and care.

Approach

We believe in providing what is best for each client. To do that, we incorporate each of the following in every program and service we offer.

Applied Behaviour Analysis

A scientific evidence-based discipline that systematically applies interventions based on the principles of learning theory to improve socially significant behaviours to a meaningful degree.

Data Collection

All of our staff are trained and required to record data to ensure decisions for each client are data driven and beneficial for them.

Staff Training

Central to providing exceptional services to our clients is ensuring our staff are adequately trained. All staff are trained in approved crisis intervention programs, first-aid, CPR, Ministry Quality Assurance Measures, and Ethics.

Beliefs & Values

At our residential centers, we believe in client first services and pride ourselves in providing the very best to all of our clients!

Our promise to our clients and their families always includes ...

  • Ensuring our client's needs come first
  • Maintaining our client's dignity
  • Incorporating our client's personal choice, encouraging independence and inclusion within their community
  • Collaborating with families, caregivers, professionals and community partners
  • Individualizing each client's programs to meet their specific needs
  • Using evidence-based programs and practices
  • Training our staff in the latest and best programs
  • Abiding by Ministry requirements
