Programs & Services

We offer year-round programs that provide comprehensive and all-inclusive curriculums to teach the necessary skills to each client. Our client's quality of life is the central focus in all of programs.

Residential Services

Our residential services are provided to individuals who cannot live at home or independently. We pride ourselves in offering exceptional full-time care and treatment programs to enhance our client's quality of life.

As part of our residential services program, clients benefit from a wide range of personalized supports, including:

  • Separate living space
  • Meals & Snacks
  • Fitness Programs
  • Community Outings
  • Life Skills Program
  • Behavioural Programs

Respite Services

Respite care offers families a much-needed break — whether for a weekend, during a crisis, or as part of ongoing support. At our residential centers, we partner with families and agencies to create flexible, compassionate respite programs tailored to each individual's needs. Every respite guest has access to the same caring staff, safe environment, and enriching programs as our full-time residents.

Crisis Support Services

Our residential centers are equipped to support individuals with developmental disabilities who need urgent, full-time care in a safe and stable environment. With a team of trained staff and expert consultants, we respond with compassion, structure, and support when families need it most.

The importance of this kind of care was highlighted in a 2016 report by the Ontario Ombudsman, calling for greater responsiveness to individuals in crisis — a call we are proud to answer.

Day Program & Vocational Skills Training

Our day program is designed and overseen by accredited behaviour analysts and offers various programs to teach life skills to our clients. All of our programs are individualized for each client to help them live meaningful lives within their community.

We teach functional abilities across various areas, including:

  • Job Preparation & Maintenance
  • Street and Personal Safety
  • Money Management
  • Household Management
  • Cooking & Nutritional Skills
  • Personal Care & Hygiene
  • Social Skills
  • Accessing & Using Community Services
  • Development of Recreation and Leisure Pursuits

Caregiver Training

To help clients be successful in their homes and with their families, we offer training to caregivers to help them maintain the skills learnt at our center. Caregiver training is available to all of our clients' registered in any program and any other interested families.
